# 🍎 NutriSense - Track your daily food intake easily

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-NutriSense-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Hectornoncommercial727/NutriSense/releases)

NutriSense helps you monitor your nutrition goals. This application provides a simple interface to log meals, track calories, and view trends without complex data entry. Users manage their health data directly on their local machine.

## 🛠 Features

NutriSense includes tools for daily food management.

*   **Meal Logging:** Enter breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.
*   **Calorie Tracking:** View total calories consumed each day.
*   **Nutrient Breakdown:** See grams of fat, protein, and carbohydrates.
*   **Personal Goals:** Set targets based on your activity level.
*   **Data Export:** Save your history as a file for your doctor.
*   **Privacy:** All data stays on your computer.

## 💻 System Requirements

NutriSense runs on Windows. Your computer needs these items:

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Memory:** At least 4 gigabytes of RAM.
*   **Storage:** 200 megabytes of free space.

## 🚀 How to Use NutriSense

Open the program using the icon on your desktop. The main screen shows your current daily progress.

### Logging Food
Click the "Add" button to document a meal. Type the name of the food and the amount in the boxes provided. The app calculates the total nutrition based on standard values. Press "Save" to add the item to your diary.

### Setting Goals
Go to the "Settings" menu to change your daily intake goals. Input your preferred calorie and nutrient targets. The app saves these settings for future sessions.

### Viewing Reports
Click the "History" tab to see your progress across weeks or months. The graph shows trends to help you understand your eating habits.
